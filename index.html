About the Lab

We focus on developing sustainable and cost-effective technologies for water and wastewater treatment, including adsorption, clay minerals, activated carbon, and zero-valent iron systems.

Research Areas

  • Adsorption of pharmaceuticals and emerging pollutants
  • Natural and modified clay materials
  • Activated carbon from biomass
  • Zero-valent iron (ZVI) systems
  • Permeable Reactive Barriers (PRB)

Methods & Analysis

  • Batch & Column experiments
  • HPLC analysis
  • ICP-OES
  • FTIR / XRD / TGA
  • Adsorption isotherms & kinetics
